GTIO Redirects:

Open a Browse Page to The GAP:
requested url: gtio://browse/brands/gap
actual url: gtio://browse/.rest.v3.outfits.brands.gap
Tested and working.
NOTE:
You can open a browse page to any api endpoint by taking the endpoint as a string (i.e. "/rest/v3/outfits/brands/gap"), replacing the slashes with dots ('.'), and appending it to the "gtio://browse/" url.
